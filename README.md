## Key Business Questions
- Which countries and cities have the highest customer concentration?
- Which customers contribute the most to total revenue?
- How do rental rates, rental duration, film length, and replacement costs vary across the catalog?

## Analysis Highlights
- Customers are concentrated in a small number of key countries, with further clustering at the city level.
- A small group of loyal customers contributes a significant share of total revenue.
- Rental pricing and duration show stable average values, indicating a balanced rental strategy with room for optimization.

## Outcome
The findings support city-level marketing strategies, targeted loyalty programs for high-value customers, and potential optimization of rental pricing and inventory management.
